Educated

"Educated" is a gripping memoir by Tara Westover, recounting her journey from a childhood of isolation and abuse in a strict Idaho family to her pursuit of education and empowerment. Despite never attending school, Tara's determination leads her to self-educate and gain admission to prestigious universities, challenging the beliefs and values that once defined her world. This inspiring and deeply moving narrative explores the transformative power of education and the strength it brings to break free from one's past.
