Foundation

"Foundation" by Isaac Asimov is a groundbreaking science fiction novel that follows mathematician Hari Seldon's vision to preserve knowledge and shorten the inevitable dark age that awaits the collapse of the Galactic Empire. Seldon establishes the Foundation on a remote planet, where he orchestrates a plan to guide humanity's future over a span of centuries. As political intrigue, scientific discoveries, and social upheavals unfold, "Foundation" captivates readers with its intricate plot and explores the complexities of predicting and shaping the destiny of civilizations.
